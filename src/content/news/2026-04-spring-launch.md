We are launching the spring 2026 cycle with three priorities: digital safety
training in every region, a transparent donation pipeline, and a fully
decentralized publishing model — every piece of content lives as a plain
text file in this repository.

This site no longer requires a database, an account, or any backend. It can
be deployed to any static host, mirrored on IPFS, or pinned to Arweave.
