# Five habits

1. **Lock everything**: phone PIN of 6+ digits, biometrics off in tense
   moments (police can compel a fingerprint, not a password in many places).
2. **Use a password manager**: Bitwarden is free and open source.
3. **Two-factor authentication everywhere** — but use an app, not SMS.
4. **Strip metadata from photos** before posting. Most platforms keep GPS.
5. **Keep a panic plan**: know how to wipe a device fast and where to find
   help offline.

When in doubt, ask in the Telegram channel. Never share IDs, addresses, or
real-time locations of fellow members.
