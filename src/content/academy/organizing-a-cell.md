# Cell template

A cell is **5 to 9 people**. Smaller is fragile, larger leaks.

Roles to rotate every 90 days:
- **Coordinator** — runs meetings, owns the agenda
- **Safety lead** — knows the legal hotline, the medic, the meeting points
- **Communications** — manages the Telegram bridge, never the only voice
- **Logistics** — water, transport, first aid

Meet weekly, in person if safe, otherwise on a voice call. Take no minutes
that name members. Decisions are made by consent: silence is not consent.
