# In one minute

- You have the right to remain silent. State your name and nothing else.
- Ask if you are free to leave. If yes, leave calmly. If no, you are
  detained — ask the legal reason.
- Do not unlock your phone. Do not consent to searches.
- Do not sign anything without a lawyer present.
- The legal hotline is staffed 24/7.
